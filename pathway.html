Pathway To The World

Sampoerna University has partnered with Broward College, located in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, USA, to co-develop U.S. accredited curriculum and provide digital academic resources during the first two years of university. Sampoerna University is proud to host a Broward College International Center on its campus the only institution in Indonesia that is accredited by a U.S. accreditation body. This allows students the flexibility to earn up to 60 credits and an associate’s degree. Credits earned can be applied to degrees at SU, the two degree program offered by SU and the University of Arizona, or they can transfer to universities in the United States or abroad to pursue the major of their choice. For more information about the Broward College International Center, visit Broward College here.

No matter your desired academic or career path, Sampoerna University provides the best options for students to graduate with a degree that ensures their success nationally and globally.
